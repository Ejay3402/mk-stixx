Welcome to NextLevelYourself: The Ultimate Drum Camp Experience Ready to take your drumming to the next level?

Why Join NextLevelYourself?

Unleash Your Full Potential This isn't just another drum camp. It's a carefully designed program to push your boundaries, refine your technique, and unlock new levels of creativity. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced drummer, you'll leave with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to perform and create with excellence. Learn from the Best MK Stixx is more than a seasoned professional; he's a mentor who's dedicated to bringing out the best in every student. Through personalized instruction, live demonstrations, and a wealth of practical advice, you’ll gain insights you won’t find anywhere else. A Global Community of Drummers Drumming is universal, and so is our camp. NextLevelYourself brings together drummers from all over the world, creating an environment rich in culture, diversity, and support. You’ll not only enhance your drumming but forge lifelong connections with fellow musicians who share your passion. Real-World Applications It’s not just about technique; it’s about playing with purpose. In this camp, you’ll learn how to apply what you’ve learned in real-world situations, whether you're performing in a band, recording in the studio, or jamming with friends. You’ll get the tools to build your personal style and take your drumming to any stage, anywhere in the world. Exclusive Access & Hands-On Training With limited spots available, NextLevelYourself offers you personalized attention and exclusive access to high-level drum techniques.
